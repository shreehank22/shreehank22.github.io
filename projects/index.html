RL Framework for Adaptive Control of Quadrotor UAVs May – Aug 2025

Developed and trained actor-critic TD3 and DDPG algorithms for dynamic adjustment of controller gains in a Quadrotor UAV simulation. Devised a reward function based on LQG cost to guide the agent towards optimal performance through iterative learning. Exhibited disturbance rejection under external perturbations. Achieved 60% lower cumulative tracking error (IAE, ISE) compared to fixed-gain and model-based controllers including PID, LQR, and LMPC.

Nonlinear Control of Cruise Missiles using Fuzzy-Tuned LQR Mar – Jun 2024

Derived a quasi-linearized fourth-order inner loop model from a nonlinear longitudinal missile autopilot using Feedback Linearization. Designed a Mamdani Type-1 fuzzy inference system in MATLAB for online adaptation of LQR weights (Q and R) in response to varying trajectories. Enhanced autopilot robustness by 28.92% compared to offline tuning approaches.
